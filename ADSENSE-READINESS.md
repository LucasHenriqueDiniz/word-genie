## Contingency Plan (If Rejected)

### Common Rejection Reasons & Fixes:
- **"Insufficient content"** → Add 2-3 more blog posts (~1500 words each)
- **"Low traffic"** → Share with gaming communities, wait 2-4 weeks
- **"Policy violations"** → Review content, clarify disclaimers
- **"Domain not trusted"** → Wait additional 30 days, reapply
- **"Technical issues"** → Check robots.txt, sitemap, indexation

### Reapplication Timeline:
- If rejected: Wait 1-2 weeks, fix issues, reapply
- Most rejections are resolved on second application
- Email Google AdSense support if stuck
