Frequently Asked Questions

How do the game solvers differ? expand_more

Gartic solvers focus on linguistic patterns, character counts, and revealed letters in a linear format. The Guess The Build solver is optimized for visual themes and categorized building prompts common in creative sandbox games.

How do I use the solvers effectively? expand_more

Simply enter the word length and any letters that have been revealed on your screen. The Genie will instantly filter through thousands of possibilities to give you the most likely matches for the current round.

Is it free to use? expand_more

Yes! Word Genie provides unlimited access to all solver databases for both Gartic and Guess The Build themes without any hidden costs or accounts required.
