Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Gartic word solver?

expand_more

A Gartic word solver is a tool designed to help players identify words based on the number of letters and any revealed characters provided during a game of Gartic.io or similar drawing games.

How do I use the pattern matcher for Guess Build?

expand_more

Simply enter the known letters in their correct positions and use an underscore (_) for every unknown character. For example, if the word is 5 letters long and you know it starts with 'H' and ends with 'O', enter H___O.

Is Word Genie free to use?

expand_more

Yes! Word Genie is 100% free. Our goal is to provide functional minimalism for gamers without charging fees or requiring subscriptions.

Can I use this for crosswords?

expand_more

Absolutely. While optimized for Gartic, the pattern matching engine works perfectly for crosswords, Scrabble, and any other letter-based word games.

Does Word Genie support other languages?

expand_more

Currently, Word Genie features a comprehensive English dictionary. We are working on expanding our linguistic database to include Portuguese, Spanish, and French soon.
